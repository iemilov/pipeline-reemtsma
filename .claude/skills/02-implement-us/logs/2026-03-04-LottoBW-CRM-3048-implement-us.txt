## Requirements
- As a customer service agent, I want to see the original sender email on the Case
- The email must be visible independent of the mapped Account's email
- Must not be overwritten by Account data
- Must be permanently traceable

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Original sender email saved on every incoming Case
2. Visible as clearly labeled field on the Case
3. Unchanged when Case mapped to Account with different email
4. Permanently traceable after edits/status changes
5. Distinguishable from Account email

## Step 2: Codebase Analysis
- SuppliedEmail standard field already exists and is auto-populated by Email-to-Case
- Field is already on all 10 Case page layouts as Readonly
- 13 permission sets already grant read access to SuppliedEmail
- STLG_CustomerService has editable=false, readable=true (correct)
- No custom field needed — SuppliedEmail meets all acceptance criteria

## Step 3: Implementation Approach — Declarative (Layout Only)
- Problem: SuppliedEmail was buried in "System Information" sidebar section
- Solution: Move SuppliedEmail to Case Information section, directly after ContactEmail
- This makes both emails visible side by side for easy comparison
- No new fields, no flows, no Apex — pure FlexiPage layout change
